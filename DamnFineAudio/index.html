About us

Damn Fine Audio is committed to providing the premium live sound experience that all artists, venues, and individuals deserve, at a price point that all can afford. Damn Fine Audio is local artist owned and operated. We strive to best serve the local art community that we are so grateful to be members of. From trivia nights to raves to house shows to wedding receptions, Damn Fine Audio is here to ensure that your voice gets heard.
